A Little about Amelia

Amelia Legge is a first-year student in the Multimedia Production program focused on graphic design and coding. In her spare time, she loves to take photos to improve her Illustrator skills to build her graphic design portfolio. Amelia is fluent and able to speak, read, and write French. She has one beautiful baby girl, who she loves very much. Outside of school she likes to spend time with her husband and daughter in the mountains, helping him work on vehicles, play with her daughter, video games, and board games and read.

She would love to travel back to where she was born and visit her grandmother in Winsford, England. She would also like to visit the Bram Castle in Romania, The British Museum in London, The Louvre, and The Eiffel Tower in France. She also enjoys watching movies and shows from the old black-and-white classics to the latest releases. Her mom and sister own a custom apparel business which also allows her to practice and improve her graphic design and Illustrator skills regularly.

Amelia loves to use movie's, book's and TV Shows to bring her inspiration for using Illustrator and Photoshop for creating scene's that never happend, or creating new backgrounds. Amelia also enjoys photoshoping her daughter, Charlotte, into movies like Jurassic Park.
